Good to know

The Sprint Race is a local time trial event on the tarmac paths around Käpplunda in Skövde.

The course is short, technical, and designed for quick racing with flowing corners, accelerations, and rhythm.

Participants race individually against the clock.

The event is designed to be simple, social, and open for both beginners and experienced riders.

Warm up starts at 09:00.

Location

The event takes place near the lakes behind Käpplundaskolan in Skövde.

Rules and Gear

The race is an individual time trial. Each participant starts alone and races against the clock.

  • Helmet is required for all participants.
  • Bicycle, running or anything on wheels/li>
  • Follow the marked course at all times.
  • Do not cut corners or leave the track.
  • Ride safely and within your own ability.
  • Respect participants, spectators, and park visitors.
  • Follow instructions from marshals and organizers.
  • Start when your name or number is called.
  • There are no prizes — only honor.
  • The fastest time wins.
  • Bring your own water and food.
  • The event is free of charge.
