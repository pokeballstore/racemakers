About the Race

The Käpplunda Sprint Race is a local time trial event on the tarmac paths around Käpplunda in Skövde.

The course is short, technical, and made for quick racing with turns, accelerations, and focus.

Everything on wheels is allowed. Each participant races individually against the clock.

This makes the event simple, fun, and open for both beginners and more experienced riders.

Warm up starts at 09:00.

Location

The event will be near the lake behind Käpplunda Skola in Skövde.

Open map

Rules and Gear

The race is an individual time trial. Each participant starts alone and races against the clock.

  • Helmet is required for all participants.
  • Everything on wheels is allowed.
  • Follow the marked course at all times.
  • Do not cut corners or leave the track.
  • Respect participants, spectators, and park visitors.
  • Follow instructions from marshals and organizers.
  • Start when your name or number is called.
  • There are no prizes — only honor.
  • The fastest time wins.
  • Bring your own water and food.
  • The event is free of charge.
