About Next Race

The Käpplunda Sprint Race is a fast and exciting event held on the tarmac paths around Käpplunda in Skövde.

The course is short, technical, and perfect for high-speed racing with tight turns and quick accelerations.

Everything on wheels is allowed, and the race is run as individual time trials.

Each participant races against the clock, making it a perfect format for both beginners and experienced riders.

Warm up starts at 09:00.

Location

The event will be near the lake behind Käpplunda Skola.

Rules and Gear

The Käpplunda Sprint Race is an individual time trial on the tarmac paths around the park. Each participant races against the clock.

  • Helmet is required for all participants.
  • Everything on wheels is allowed.
  • Follow the marked course at all times.
  • Do not cut corners or leave the track.
  • Respect other participants, spectators, and park visitors.
  • Follow instructions from marshals and organizers.
  • Start when your name or number is called.
  • There are no prizes - only honor.
  • The fastest time wins.
  • Bring your own water and food.
  • Free of charge.
