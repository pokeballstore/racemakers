About Racemakers

A local outdoor community built around movement, challenge, and shared experiences.

Who We Are

Racemakers started as a group of friends who enjoy being outside, staying active, creating events, and doing things together.

Racing and wheels are part of the identity, but Racemakers is not only about speed. It is about movement, community, and finding new ways to make outdoor activities more exciting.

The goal is to create a welcoming environment where people can meet, challenge themselves, compete in a friendly way, and enjoy time outdoors.

What We Do

Racemakers organizes simple and social outdoor activities with a focus on participation, energy, and community.

Events can include sprint races, café rides, hiking activities, endurance challenges, social meetups, and other outdoor gatherings.

Some events are competitive, while others are more relaxed. The common idea is always the same: get outside, move, meet people, and make the activity feel like something worth showing up for.

Community

Racemakers is built around the idea that activity becomes more fun when people do it together.

You do not need to be the fastest or the most experienced person there. You can join to compete, improve your own time, try something new, or simply be part of the atmosphere.

Everyone who respects the event, the people around them, and the outdoor environment is welcome.
